Why Choose Us?

Hearing loss is a widespread issue worldwide, affecting around 360 million people globally. In India, 63 million individuals have significant hearing problems, including many children. Each year, over 100,000 babies are born with hearing issues. Adult-onset deafness affects about 7.6% of the population, while childhood onset is around 2%.

Vatsalya Speech and Hearing Clinic has been helping people with hearing difficulties for the past 20 years. Established in 2003 in Rajkot, we now serve the entire Gujarat region, providing top-notch services for speech, hearing, and Cochlear implants. Using advanced equipment, we aim to improve the lives of those affected by hearing loss across our community.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where everyone has the opportunity to communicate freely and confidently, regardless of any hearing or speech challenges they may face.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals with hearing and speech challenges by providing compassionate care, innovative solutions, and personalized support to enhance their quality of life.
